*Lectures* are talks or presentations given by someone, usually a teacher or expert, to teach or explain a specific topic to an audience, often in a classroom or academic setting.

**In English, repeat after me:**
- *Lectures* are talks or presentations meant to teach something.

**Four meanings of "lectures" in Spanish:**
1. Conferencias
2. Clases magistrales
3. Lecciones
4. Discursos

**Synonyms in English:**
- Presentations
- Talks
- Lessons
- Speeches

**Simple example:**
- *The professor gave a lecture on history.* (El profesor dio una conferencia sobre historia.)
